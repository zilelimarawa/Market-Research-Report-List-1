<p><ul><li>Cross Flow Fan Blades</li><li>Centrifugal Fan Blades</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioner fan blades market is primarily divided into two types: cross flow fan blades and centrifugal fan blades. Cross flow fan blades are designed to provide a steady airflow across a larger area, making them ideal for low-profile units and ensuring even temperature distribution. In contrast, centrifugal fan blades create a high-pressure airflow, making them suitable for applications requiring powerful air circulation, such as in larger HVAC systems, where efficient cooling is essential for comfort and performance.</p></p>
