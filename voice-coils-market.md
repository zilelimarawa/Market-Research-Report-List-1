<p><ul><li>Single Voice Coils (SVC)</li><li>Dual Voice Coils (DVC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voice coils market primarily features two types: Single Voice Coils (SVC) and Dual Voice Coils (DVC). SVC products consist of one coil, simplifying design and making them suitable for low-power applications. In contrast, DVC products feature two coils, offering flexibility in wiring configurations to handle more power and improve sound quality. DVCs are preferred in high-performance audio systems, enabling options like parallel or series connections, catering to diverse consumer preferences in the audio market.</p></p>
